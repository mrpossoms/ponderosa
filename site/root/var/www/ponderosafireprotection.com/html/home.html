The Ponderosa System

Four layers of defense.
One coordinated system.

Sprinklers alone fail when power and water cut out. Mesh alone can't stop a hot vegetation fire at your fence line. Native plants alone won't shield your roof. We combine every proven layer into one engineered defense — designed for Colorado's wind, snow, drought, and ember storms.

Layer 01

Eco-Safe Retardant Application

Komodo plant-based fire retardant applied to roof, siding, decks, and perimeter vegetation. Forms a protective barrier that resists ignition and slows fire spread — and stays effective for months until heavy rainfall.

  • Non-toxic · biodegradable · pet, pollinator, and water-system safe
  • Persists through Colorado's peak fire season
  • No PFAS, no harsh chemicals, no permanent residue
Layer 02

Ember-Blocking Stainless Mesh

Wildfire Defense Mesh installed over every ember entry point — gable vents, foundation vents, soffits, deck skirting, gutter guards, and bird-stops. Up to 98% effective at blocking embers and reduces radiant heat by ~38%.

  • Patent-pending stainless steel · ASTM E2886 tested
  • Meets California Chapter 7A — the country's strictest standard
  • Permanent. Install once. Protected for the life of your home.
Layer 03

Fire-Wise Native Landscape Design

Defensible-space zoning from 0–100 ft using Colorado-native, low-flammability plants. We remove ignition points without scorching your yard — replacing them with beautiful, drought-tolerant, fire-resistant species that thrive in our climate.

  • Native plant consultation by certified ecologists
  • Ember-resistant perimeter strategy (Zone 0, 1, 2)
  • Reduces ignition risk at the source: fuel
Layer 04

Resilient Water & Smart Activation

On-site water storage, gas-powered backup pumps, and roof + perimeter sprinkler zones that work even when the grid and city water are down. Optional smart activation triggers on temperature, smoke, or fire-proximity alerts.

  • 1,500–10,000 gal storage · grid-independent pump systems
  • Optional gravity-fed fail-safe — works without power
  • Smart triggers (heat, smoke, API alerts) on Resilience+ tiers

Common Questions

What homeowners ask first.

Is the retardant really safe for pets, plants, and water?+
Yes. We exclusively use Komodo's plant-based, biodegradable retardant. It contains no PFAS, no toxic surfactants, and is engineered to break down naturally without contaminating soil or water systems. Safe for kids, dogs, chickens, pollinators, and food gardens.
How long does the retardant last?+
A standard pre-season application persists 4–6 months under typical Colorado conditions and only washes off in a sustained, heavy rainfall. We time treatments to peak fire season (typically May–October on the Front Range, longer in mountain towns).
Does the mesh damage my home or its aesthetics?+
No. Wildfire Defense Mesh installs flush against vents and soffits, and comes in black, bronze, or white finishes that blend with most exteriors. It's permanent, low-profile, and code-compliant. Most homeowners don't notice it after install.
What happens if the power and water are out during a fire?+
That's the entire point of our Resilience and Full Defense Platform tiers. On-site water storage, gas-powered pumps, and optional gravity-fed fail-safes mean your defense system functions independent of grid power and municipal water. This is the single biggest gap in conventional sprinkler systems.
Will this lower my insurance premiums?+
Often yes. Several Colorado insurers now offer premium discounts or reverse non-renewal decisions when homeowners install hardened defense systems. We provide documentation packets you can submit to your carrier, and we're actively working with regional insurers on formal partnership programs.
Do I need permits?+
Mesh retrofits and retardant applications typically do not require permits. Roof-mounted sprinkler systems and water storage installations may require local permitting — we handle the permitting process as part of any Resilience or Full Defense install.
How quickly can you install before fire season?+
Essentials treatments take a single day. Resilience installs are typically 2–4 days. Full Defense Platform builds run 5–10 days depending on water storage scope. We prioritize properties in active red-flag conditions and book the May–June window heavily — reserve your assessment early.
